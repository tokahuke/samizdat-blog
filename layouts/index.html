The SAMIZDAT SYSTEM

Your content, available.

---

SAMIZDAT is a distributed self-publishing system. Publish and view content you love in the Internet without meddling intermediaries!

Get Samizdat View on GitHub

All Samizdat Software is Free Software and is fully available free of charge.

How it works

1.

Build your site locally, in your own computer

2.

Publish to the Samizdat network, a peer-to-peer content distribution system.

3.

People who visit your site will propagate your ideas further, using their cache.

Convenient

Forget about looking for platforms to serve your content. Deploy locally and let your biggest fans serve it for you.

Escape Big-Tech Censorship

Samizdat is a carrier, not a publisher. This is not policy; this is design.

Private

Only the parties serving and receiving content know what it is all about. No corporation-in-the-middle; no government-in-the-middle.

It's just the Web all over again,
But think of the possibilities!

By structuring the Web on content rather than on location, we subvert the pervese incentives of having to trust big, meddling corporations to serve our content. Being a server is not the privilege of the big guy anymore.
